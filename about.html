What is TotemRush?

TotemRush is a fast-paced Minecraft server where players battle using totems, kits, and custom powers in a chaotic and competitive world.

We combine hardcore PvP, custom enchantments, and exclusive cosmetics to create the ultimate rush of adrenaline. Play solo, team up, or climb the leaderboard.
